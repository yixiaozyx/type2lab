History of Chinese paper cutting
Paper-cuts are paper-cuts pasted on window paper or window glass,one of the traditional folk arts ofthe Han nationality in ancient China.It has a long history, unique style, loved by people at home andabroad.
Paper-cut is a characteristic art of farming culture. The geographical environment of rural life, agricultural production characteristics and social customs also make this local art with distinctive folktaste and artistic characteristics of the Han nationality.
